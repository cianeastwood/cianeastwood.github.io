Cian Eastwood

I'm a Senior Research Scientist at Relation, working on generative models of biological data.

I am particularly interested in diffusion/flow models and ways to achieve generalisation, multimodality, and test-time control.

Previously, I was at Valence Labs / Recursion, working on generative models for cell microscopy. I completed my PhD jointly at the University of Edinburgh (with Chris Williams) and the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems (with Bernhard Schölkopf), working on generalisation and representation learning. During my PhD, I spent time at Google DeepMind, Meta AI (FAIR) and Spotify.
